Hi, we are the Murmurs of Earth Lab.

The Murmurs of Earth are two phonograph records that were included aboard both Voyager spacecraft launched in 1977.The records contain sounds and images selected to portray the diversity of life and culture on Earth, and are intended for any intelligent extraterrestrial life form, or for future humans, who may find them. The records are a sort of time capsule.
Working with painting, screen printing, motion graphic and animation, their focus on colour, form, flat shapes and lines tell narrative stories to the audience about the romantic world in their mind and the universe.

我们的故事

我们是Murmurs of Earth实验室

1977年，旅行者号带着一张名叫Murmurs of Earth的唱片飞向宇宙，其中记载了35种来自地球的声音，电闪雷鸣，野兽咆哮，婴孩诞生，摩斯密码等等。还收录了55种语言向未知生命的问候：“行星地球的孩子向你们问好”。 旅行者号将在2025年电池耗尽，和人类彻底失联，而这张唱片也将孤独得在宇宙中流浪，变成一封等待未知文明查收的信。
而我们，想用艺术的方式记录下我们想对这个世界，对宇宙，对未知所说的话。从独立制作的zine，到丝网印刷的插画，到以Murmurs of Earth为主题设计的系列丝巾，我们用我们所能的形式，告诉这个世界我们的探索和思考。
